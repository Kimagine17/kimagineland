Plarn Projects!

Thanks for visiting Kimfire's plarn projects!

  Plarn is an portmanteau of plastic and yarn. Using plarn is a great way to help save the environment! This basket was made by saving bread and bagel bags from the store and took several months of collecting to create.

  Here are some great reasons consider to use plarn:

  • Plastic is readily avaible for use
  • Plarn has a unique style and texture
  • It's a great way to upcycle
  • Plarn is very strong and sturdy

Next time you go grocery shopping, save those bags!
